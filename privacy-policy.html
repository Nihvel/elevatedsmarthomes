Cookie Policy & Data Privacy Statement

Welcome to Elevated Smart Homes. We believe in absolute transparency regarding your privacy.

This website is designed with a fundamental commitment to not collect any personal data from its visitors. Juri Calleri, the operator of Elevated Smart Homes, ensures that your visit remains private.

Here’s what that means for you:

  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or process any personal data from you, such as your IP address, Browse habits, or demographic information.
  • No Tracking Technologies: We do not use cookies, web beacons, tracking pixels, or any other tracking technologies to monitor your activity on this site or across other websites.
  • No Analytics: We do not use analytics tools (like Google Analytics, Matomo, etc.) that would collect information about your visit.
  • No Third-Party Embeds: This website does not embed content from third-party services (such as social media feeds, video players, or external maps) that could potentially transfer data to those third parties.
  • Locally Hosted Assets: All fonts, scripts, and other design elements are hosted directly on our server. This ensures that no data is shared with external content delivery networks (CDNs) or font providers.

Regarding "Cookies" and GitHub Pages:

This website, hosted on GitHub Pages, is designed not to set any cookies from our side. However, GitHub, as the hosting provider, may use essential, technically necessary cookies for the operation and security of their platform (e.g., for load balancing or security features). These are related to the hosting service itself and are not used by us to track or identify you. As these are outside of our direct control, we recommend reviewing GitHub's privacy policy for more details on their infrastructure practices.

Your Data, Your Control:

Since we collect no personal data, there is no data of yours for us to store, process, or share. Your visit to this website remains entirely private.
